Juggling isn’t just circus fun—it sharpens ​your focus, boosts hand-eye coordination, and gives you a sense of playful confidence you can use on any stage

Step into the magical world of juggling with Fred Kroket! In this fun, hands-on workshop you’ll learn the core moves every juggler needs, build coordination and stage confidence—and leave with a brand-new party trick under your belt. Whether you’ve never touched a juggling club or just want to polish your routine, Fred’s playful teaching style makes it easy and memorable.

What You Will Learn

  • Basisoefeningen voor statisch evenwicht (staan op één been, hiel-teen-stand)
  • Dynamisch evenwicht (lopen op een plank, werken op een evenwichtsbalk)
  • Onstabiele ondergronden (wiebelborden, slacklines)
  • Overgangen, draaien & langzame “flow”-reeksen

 Class details

  • Duration: starting from 45 minutes
  • Group size: up to 30 people
  • What to bring: comfy clothes
  • Location: Gymnasium, kinderspeelzaal, big room

Who It’s For

  • Absolute beginners (no experience needed!)
  • Kids & teens (ages 8+)
  • Adults looking for a new party trick
  • Team-building groups
  • Special education
